About Norwood Gulf

Our Mission:
Excellence & Education.

From over 80 years of experience, we offer all needs on behalf of customer requests. With the addition of State Inspection Stickers to our auto repair business, we are committed to exceeding our customers expectations.

We believe in transparency and education. When you bring your vehicle to us, we don't just fix problems — we explain them in clear, understandable terms. You'll know exactly what work needs to be done, why it's necessary, and what it will cost before we begin.

Our business values honesty, quality, and customer education above all else. We aim to build long-term trust with every person who brings their car to our shop, creating relationships that last for years.
